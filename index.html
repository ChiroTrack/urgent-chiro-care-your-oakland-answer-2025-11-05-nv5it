About The Shift Chiropractic

Founded in 2008, The Shift Chiropractic began with a simple mission: to revolutionize emergency chiropractic care in Oakland. Our founder, Dr. Marcus Chen, recognized a critical gap in the healthcare system—patients suffering from acute pain with nowhere to turn outside of traditional hospital emergency rooms. Frustrated by the lack of specialized chiropractic emergency services, he established The Shift to provide immediate, expert care for those experiencing sudden spinal injuries, acute pain, and musculoskeletal emergencies. Over the past 15 years, we've grown from a single-doctor practice to a comprehensive emergency care facility, treating thousands of patients and earning a reputation as Oakland's most trusted urgent chiropractic provider.

Today, The Shift Chiropractic stands on the foundation of compassion, expertise, and innovation. Our core values—putting patients first, maintaining the highest standards of clinical excellence, and embracing continuous education—guide every decision we make. We believe that quality emergency care should be accessible, affordable, and delivered with genuine care. Our team of board-certified chiropractors undergoes continuous training in advanced techniques, emergency protocols, and patient-centered care. We're committed to not just treating symptoms, but addressing root causes, empowering our patients with knowledge and tools for long-term wellness, and building a healthier Oakland community one adjusted spine at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our emergency chiropractic services

If you experience acute injury or severe pain, contact The Shift Chiropractic immediately. We offer same-day emergency appointments and extended hours to accommodate urgent situations. Upon arrival, our team will conduct a rapid assessment to determine the severity of your condition and provide immediate care. If necessary, we can coordinate with other medical professionals. Do not delay seeking care—early intervention is crucial for optimal recovery.

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. We work directly with insurance companies to maximize your coverage and minimize out-of-pocket costs. We also offer flexible payment plans for uninsured patients and accept all major credit cards, cash, and digital payment methods. During your initial consultation, we'll review your insurance coverage and discuss payment options to find the best solution for your situation.

Initial emergency appointments typically take 45-60 minutes. This includes a comprehensive assessment, imaging if necessary, the initial adjustment, and discussion of your treatment plan. Follow-up appointments are usually 20-30 minutes. We prioritize efficiency without compromising quality care. In true emergencies, we can provide rapid assessment and immediate relief in 20-30 minutes, with comprehensive evaluation following once your acute symptoms are addressed.

Chiropractic care is safe for most people when performed by qualified professionals. We treat patients from infants to seniors with specialized techniques appropriate for each age group. During your consultation, we'll discuss your complete medical history, current conditions, and medications to ensure safe, personalized care. In rare cases where traditional adjustments aren't appropriate, we offer alternative therapies like therapeutic massage, traction, or specialized exercises to achieve your wellness goals.

Most patients experience significant pain relief immediately after adjustment. Some may experience mild soreness for 24-48 hours as muscles adjust—this is normal and typically resolves quickly. We provide detailed home care instructions including ice/heat therapy, specific exercises, and activity modifications. Recovery timelines vary: acute injuries often resolve in 2-6 weeks with proper care, while chronic conditions may require longer treatment. We'll schedule follow-up appointments and monitor your progress closely to ensure optimal recovery.
